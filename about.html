About Us

we believe that every loaf tells a story and every pastry carries a little joy. Nestled in the heart of our community, we’re more than just a bakery—we’re a daily ritual, a place where the aroma of fresh bread and sweet treats brings people togethe!

Fresh Ingredients
Delicious Bread

Our breads are handcrafted with care, from the slow-fermented sourdough with its golden crust and tangy bite, to the pillowy-soft milk loaves that melt in your mouth. Each batch is kneaded with love and baked to bring out deep, rich flavors and a heavenly aroma that fills the air.

Our Story

One Bakery began with a simple dream: to bring the warmth of homemade bread and the joy of fresh pastries to every table. What started as a humble kitchen experiment quickly grew into a neighborhood favorite, thanks to our unwavering love for baking and the belief that good food brings people together..
